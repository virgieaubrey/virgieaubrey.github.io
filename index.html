About us



Colossal Piano Lessons provides an enjoyable yet impactful learning for by our certified instructors. Since 2024, We have provided care and attention for our students. From toddlers to junior high school students, kids from 5 up to 12 are warmly welcomed to join our service.

Lesson Plan



We ensure that our practice will be suitable for each of our students' abilities. A free trial lesson is provided in order to measure our kids' current talent. After placing learners in their current grade, kids are able to expand their capabilities and take their monthly test to ascend toward a higher level.
